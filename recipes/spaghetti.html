Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is the ultimate classic Italian comfort food, featuring long strands of pasta tossed in a rich, hearty meat sauce with tangy tomatoes and savory herbs. This homemade spaghetti bolognese serves 4-6 people and takes about 1 hour total.

Serve with a fresh Caesar salad and crusty garlic bread for a perfect family meal!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato paste
  • Basil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil for the pasta.
  2. For the meat sauce, brown 1 pound of ground beef (or a mix of beef and Italian sausage) in a large skillet over medium heat, then add 1 diced onion and 3 minced garlic cloves, cooking until softened.
  3. Stir in a 28-ounce can of crushed tomatoes, 2 tablespoons tomato paste, 1 teaspoon dried basil, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.
  4. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, to let the flavors meld.
  5. Meanwhile, add the spaghetti to the boiling water and cook according to package directions until al dente (usually 8-10 minutes).
  6. Drain the pasta, reserving a cup of pasta water.
  7. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the meat sauce, adding a splash of reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
  8. Serve immediately, topped with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves. Enjoy hot!
