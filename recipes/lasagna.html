Lasagna

Decsription

Lasagna is the ultimate comfort food, featuring layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey melted cheese. This classic version serves 8-10 people and takes about 1.5 hours total (including baking time).

Serve with a side salad and garlic bread for a perfect family meal!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato paste
  • Basil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. For the meat sauce, brown 1 pound of ground beef (or a mix of beef and Italian sausage) in a large skillet over medium heat, then add 1 diced onion and 3 minced garlic cloves, cooking until softened.
  3. Stir in a 28-ounce can of crushed tomatoes, 2 tablespoons tomato paste, 1 teaspoon dried basil, 1 teaspoon dried oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.
  4. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix 15 ounces ricotta cheese with 1 egg, 1/4 cup grated Parmesan, and 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley.
  6. Add a layer of no-boil lasagna noodles (or cooked regular noodles), followed by ricotta mixture, shredded mozzarella (about 3 cups total for the dish), and more meat sauce.
  7. Repeat the layers 3-4 times, ending with meat sauce topped with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then uncover and bake another 25 minutes until bubbly and golden. Let it rest for 15 minutes before slicing to allow the layers to set.
