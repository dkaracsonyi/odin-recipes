Pizza

Description

Pizza is the ultimate crowd-pleasing dish, featuring a crispy thin crust, tangy tomato sauce, melted mozzarella, and your favorite toppings. This classic homemade version makes one 12-inch pizza (serves 2-4 people) and takes about 1 hour total (including baking time), plus dough rising time.

Serve with a fresh green salad or antipasto for a perfect casual meal!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings (e.g., pepperoni, basil, mushrooms, olives)
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) or as high as it goes, ideally with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside to heat up.
  2. If using homemade dough, prepare it in advance: mix 2 cups flour, 1 teaspoon yeast, 1 teaspoon salt, and 3/4 cup warm water; knead and let rise for 1 hour.
  3. Roll or stretch the dough into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface or parchment paper.
  4. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle generously with shredded mozzarella cheese, then add your chosen toppings like pepperoni slices and fresh basil leaves.
  6. Drizzle lightly with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and dried oregano if desired.
  7. Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated stone or baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and bubbly, and the cheese is melted and lightly browned. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
